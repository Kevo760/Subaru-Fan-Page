The Generations

Mean Eye Bug Eye Blob Eye Hawk Eye Stink Eye Evo Eye

Mean Eye

Not a suprise that we start with the 1st genaration WRX/STI, We choose this image because how clean and simple the car looks. It has a wide body style to it and the wheel fitment is on point. You will also notice this car is using its AWD to its fullest by not being super low!

Bug Eye

Bumble Bee is that you? We picked this because of how eye catching the color is and the gloss black front lip. 57DR wheels with the red hub centric caps make it stand out. The huge fog lights match with the body style.

Blob Eye

This one was hard to choose, but we ended up picking two Blob Eyes. These both look clean in white, another one with a widebody and the other rocking the stock wide body look! The left is specially unique for it wheels and fog light bezel

Hawk Eye

The hawkeye is one of the favorite WRX/STI models, in our opinion is to keep the bumper stock. It does NOT need a aftermarket front bumper. This blacked out Hawk Eye is rocking the OG RPF1s. Suprisingly the chrome headlights match with the black.

Stink Eye

Wide body for lyfe! This Stink eye is the widest of them all! Having a wide body looks so satisfying with the hatchback models. It's like peanut butter with jelly, gotta have them both! The hood looks aggresive and the blackout top makes it look so good!

Evo Eye

Finally not a widebody WRX/STI! This one is overall clean and looks like a style I would rock! Clean 57CR wheels that match with the break calipers, Functional airduck fog light covers, and an aggressive lip kit!
